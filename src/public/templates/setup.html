What is Nintendo TVii?

Nintendo TVii is an platform that allows you to find and search for TV shows, movies and actors in Streaming platforms and Live TV. Along with that, it also offers a diverse variety of social features, such as TV Tag.


What is TV Tag?

While you are watching a new episode of a live TV show, the TV Tag feature (if supported) will allow you to comment on live TV moments with other people as they happen, with a short caption and an attached image/poll/etc. that makes watching TV entertaining and social. Along with commenting, you can also doodle on this attached image, and the comments you do are crossposted to other social media such as Miiverse, Bluesky and Tumblr (if set before)


What can i do with Nintendo TVii?

Nintendo TVii offers you the option to see your Favorites, TV shows airing live, or releasing new episodes soon on Streaming platforms, Movies also shows you the currently airing movies live, or the popular/trending ones on Streaming platforms, Sports allows you to see the upcoming Sports games, and see live stats, player positions, and scores with TV Tag, TV Tag allows you see the upcoming and completed programs that supported TV Tag, and you can interact with other people in these taglines, as seen in the previous question.
